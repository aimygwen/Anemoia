Visual Artist
That's me
Maker of this mess. ︎

I focus on visuals that do more than exist aesthetically. Built with intention, they carry meaning, communicate ideas, and tell stories. Across different forms, driven by same curiosity and obsession.

Born in the late 90s, made in Germany. When I stumbled pixel art at very early age, I learned how digital art didn’t just widen the horizon, it made it infinite. A borderless space where the only limit is your imagination and stamina.

My work spans 2D illustration, character and environmental art, animation, and design. I’ve been chasing the pretty mess for over sixteen years. Self-taught. Drawn to friction, transition, and the beauty inside instability. Memory is messy. I just make the mess look intentional.

Shaped by the world ︎

I’m shaped by the world around me. By details, moods, and experiences that make life feel more vivid. These influences accumulate and resurface through my work, transforming inspiration into output.

What’s
Next?

I’m moving toward broader forms of creation. Recently, my practice has expanded into low-poly 3D modeling, alongside a renewed focus on pixel-based work. To move forward, I returned to my roots and reshaped them into something new. I want to keep learning across disciplines, including programming and new technical forms, to build immersive worlds that move, breathe, and overflow with color and joy, spaces that inspire others the way art once inspired me.
