The face behind the creations

That's me. The maker of this mess. :D

Sometimes it’s low-poly worlds, sometimes it’s illustration, animation, or design—but it’s always about the "in-between."

For sixteen years, my favorite places have been the ones that don't belong to a specific moment. I love liminality: the quiet, blurry lines where things transform into something new. To me, the journey we're all on isn't a path to a final destination. The journey is the whole point. Being self-taught means my process is guided entirely by curiosity and intuition. I spend my time looking for meaning in forgotten corners and turning abstract, fleeting feelings into something you can see and hold onto. I don't want my art to just sit quietly on a screen. I want it to leave a lingering feeling behind.

Come along!

It doesn't need to be perfect. It just needs to be. So make it exist.

You can obsess over trying to perfect everything until you're numb, but never forget what inspires you to create in the first place.
